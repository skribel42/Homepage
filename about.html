Who I Am

I'm Robert Alexander — a Security Engineer with a passion for understanding how things break. I hold CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ certifications and have completed Harvard's CS50. My career is headed toward offensive security and penetration testing, and I spend a lot of my time sharpening those skills both professionally and on my own time.

I believe the best defenders think like attackers. That mindset drives everything I do.

>

Professional

I work in cybersecurity doing the kind of IT work that keeps businesses running and secured — endpoint management, Microsoft 365, network security, and incident response. I'm the person who finds a way in when nobody else can.

I actively practice offensive security skills on TryHackMe and am working toward enrolling in OffSec's OSCP (Learn One) program — the gold standard penetration testing certification.
